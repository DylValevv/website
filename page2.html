Howdy. My name's Dylan Valev.
I'm an experimental VFX artist seeking to push the boundaries of interactive media. I also make games.

I am very involved in my local game development community. It is here where I learned how to be a leader, spearheading the effort in the creation of WPI's International Game Developers Association chapter, where I listened to what game development students wanted, made petitions, and created many documents and meetings in order to be recognized as a club to my institution, and ultimately, as a chapter to the IGDA. As its president for two years, I lead discussions and activities, planned and ran Game Jams and workshops, and brought in guest speakers from local companies such as Demiurge Studios and MassDiGI in order to further improve the skills of my peers.

My work has been shown at PAX East 19' & 20', MassDiGI Game Challenge 19' & 20', Boston Festival of Indie Games 18' & 19', and many more events in the Greater Boston Area.

Furthermore, my work is shown in the GDC Vault for winning Gold in the 2020 Game Narrative Review Competition. Click here to read it!
